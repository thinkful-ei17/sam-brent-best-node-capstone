Restaurant Wishlist is a desktop application making it easy for you to track restaurants you'd like to visit, keep track of recommendations on dishes to try, and take notes on how it was after you visit. Simply search for a restaurant in the map feature, and click the 'Add to Wishlist' button. To the right you can edit and update your wishlist at any time.

Don't ever forget that great place you have to try again!
